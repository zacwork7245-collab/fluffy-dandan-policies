# Privacy Policy for Fluffy Dandan Automation

**Last updated: April 29, 2026**

## Overview

Fluffy Dandan Automation ("the App") is a personal content automation tool 
operated by the owner of the @fluffydandanofficial accounts across YouTube, 
Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. This Privacy Policy describes how the App 
handles data.

## Who This Applies To

This App is intended for use by its owner only. It is not a public service, 
does not accept third-party users, and does not collect or process data 
from anyone other than the App's operator.

## What Data the App Accesses

The App may access the following data through official platform APIs 
(YouTube Data API, Meta Graph API, TikTok Open API):

- The App owner's own social media accounts and Pages
- Content (videos, captions, hashtags) the App owner creates and chooses to publish
- Basic profile information (account name, ID, follower counts) of the App owner's own accounts
- Performance metrics (views, likes, comments) of the App owner's own posts

## What the App Does Not Do

- The App does not collect, store, or process any data from end users or viewers
- The App does not share data with any third parties
- The App does not use data for advertising, analytics, or marketing
- The App does not track users across services
- The App does not access data from any account other than the App owner's own

## Data Storage

Authentication tokens used to connect to platform APIs are stored locally on 
the App owner's personal computer in encrypted form. No data is transmitted 
to external servers.

## Data Retention

Authentication tokens are retained until the App owner manually revokes them. 
Content uploaded through the App is governed by each respective platform's 
own data retention policies.

## Third-Party Platforms

The App integrates with third-party platforms (YouTube, Facebook, Instagram, 
TikTok). Each platform's privacy policy governs how those platforms handle 
data:

- YouTube: https://policies.google.com/privacy
- Meta (Facebook & Instagram): https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy
- TikTok: https://www.tiktok.com/legal/privacy-policy

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect 
information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. The "Last updated" date at the top will 
reflect any changes.
